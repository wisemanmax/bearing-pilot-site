No stories are published yet. Client stories will appear here only after the current pilot cycle concludes, and only with each client's separate written consent — never as a condition of the work. Until then, this page shows the format those stories will follow, and nothing else.

How every story will be told

Each published case study will follow the same six-part format. The outline below is a template only — it contains no client information.

Template — no client information

Starting point

Where the business stood when the engagement began, described in plain terms the client has approved.

What was clarified

The findings the client chose to share, in their own words.

First move

The single action the client committed to after the debrief.

90-day measure

The measure the client chose to track, named only with their permission.

What changed

What the client observed over the following quarter, reviewed and approved by them before publication.

Client reflection

The client's own closing words, published only with their sign-off.

Publication standard

Before any story appears here, all of the following must hold:

  • The client gives separate written consent to publication — distinct from the debrief confirmation, and never a condition of the work.
  • Identifying details — the client's name, business name, and any specific figures — appear only with explicit permission for each; otherwise the story is anonymized.
  • The client reviews the full draft and approves the final wording before anything is published.
  • Consent can be withdrawn at any time, and the story comes down.
