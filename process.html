Process

Five stages, in order. None of them is a lecture — each one shines a light on what is already true in your business and hands you the page that proves it. Here is what happens, and when.

The five stages

  1. 01°

    First Look

    Free, five questions, a couple of minutes. You get an instant directional read on where the business stands — no advisor time, no obligation. It exists so you can decide whether a full reading is worth your money before you spend any.

    Take the First Look

  2. 02°

    Assessment

    When you book a First Bearing, you complete the full written assessment: plain questions about how the business actually runs and the numbers behind it. There is nothing to study for and no trick questions. If you don't know an answer, say so — a gap you can't fill is itself worth knowing about.

  3. 03°

    One-Page Truth

    An advisor reads your answers and writes your One-Page Truth: three findings, one first move, and the measure to watch over the next 90 days. It arrives within five business days of submitting your assessment — one page, in plain language, about your business and nobody else's.

  4. 04°

    75-minute debrief

    You and the advisor walk through the page together: what each finding rests on, why the first move comes first, and how to use the 90-day Growth Map after the call. It's a working session, not a presentation — you leave knowing exactly what to do on Monday.

  5. 05°

    Continuity — optional

    The First Bearing is complete on its own. If you want your bearings kept current, Continuity adds three lighter reviews through the year and an annual reassessment. It's a standing offer, not a pitch at the end of your debrief.

    See Continuity on the Services page

The promise behind the schedule

  • Your One-Page Truth arrives within five business days of submitting the assessment. If Bearing misses that window without an agreed reschedule, the First Bearing fee is refunded.
  • Every First Bearing includes one free correction for factual errors reported within seven days of delivery.
